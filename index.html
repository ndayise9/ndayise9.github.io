Background

Hi, my name is Emmanuel Ndayisaba and I am a GIS graduate based in Toronto, Ontario. I completed my undergrad at York University and graduated with a Bachelors degree in Environmental Studies. I graduated with the goal of working to protect natural spaces from further deterioaration and work toward their reclamation. It was this combination that led me to pursue a GIS certificate at Humber College to learn how to visualize and interpret geospatial data. I gained practical experience in analyzing and visualizing vector and raster data to identify and explain a variety of trends. This allowed me to gain experience researching and accessing data sources from both government sites and private providers. When I'm not creating maps or managing databases I spend my time travelling (7 countries, 4 continents and counting), reading, and learning new skills (I'm currently trying my hand at full stack development).
